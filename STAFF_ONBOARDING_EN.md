## 📱 Five Things You'll Use Every Shift

### 1. **Shift Checklist** ✅
Your daily to-do list. Auto-generated each shift.

**What it looks like:**
```
🔑 Opening Shift - Juan
├─ Check lobby temperature
├─ Restock toiletries (105-110)
├─ Send welcome messages to new guests
└─ Verify WiFi password changed
```

**How to use:**
1. Click "✅ Checklist"
2. Read item
3. When done → click item (turns green)
4. Manager sees completion in real-time

**Tips:**
- Takes 15-30 mins usually
- Mark items as you do them (not at end)
- Add notes if something is broken/weird

---

### 2. **Incident Report** 🚨
Something broken? Find it, report it.

**Examples:**
- Broken toilet in 203
- Guest complaint about noise
- Roof leak
- Missing towels

**How to use:**
1. Click "🚨 Report"
2. Take photo (phone camera)
3. Select room/area on map
4. Choose severity (Low/Medium/High/Critical)
5. Write quick description
6. Submit

**What happens next:**
- Manager gets instant notification
- Maintenance team gets assigned
- You can track status in real-time

---

### 3. **Real-time Notifications** 💬
Alerts when you're needed.

**You'll get notified for:**
- 🔔 New guest request (30-sec delay)
- 📝 Task assigned to you
- ⚠️ High-priority issue
- 💬 Direct message from manager

**How to respond:**
1. Notification pops up
2. Click to see details
3. Accept task or request
4. Mark as "In Progress"
5. When done → "Complete"

---

### 4. **Playbooks** 📚
Your operational guides. "How to..." for everything.

**Examples:**
- "How to handle a drunk guest"
- "WiFi troubleshooting"
- "Emergency procedures"
- "How to make the perfect checkin"

**How to use:**
1. Click "📚 Playbooks"
2. Search or browse
3. Follow step-by-step
4. Take photo if needed for proof

**Why it's cool:**
- Same info every time (no confusion)
- Photos + videos included
- Everyone follows same standard
- New staff learns faster

---

### 5. **Bot QA** 💬 (AI Assistant)
Ask Hostack anything.

**Questions you can ask:**
- "What's the WiFi password?"
- "Where are the clean towels?"
- "How do I report a guest complaint?"
- "What's the checkout procedure?"

**How to use:**
1. Click "💬 Bot"
2. Type question in plain English/Spanish
3. Bot responds instantly
4. Ask follow-ups if needed

## 💡 Pro Tips for Day 1

### Mobile-Friendly
- App works great on older phones
- Font sizes adjusted for small screens
- Pinch to zoom if tiny
- Long scrolls are normal (lots of content)

### Privacy
- Don't share login with other staff
- Each person gets own account
- Manager can see who did what
- This is actually good (accountability)

### Common Mistakes
❌ Don't wait until end of shift to do checklist
✅ Do items as you go

❌ Don't write novels in incident reports
✅ Keep it short: "Room 305 shower handle broken"

❌ Don't ignore notifications
✅ Check every 15 mins or enable sounds

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**"I can't log in"**
- Wait 1 min after clicking invite link
- Try different browser
- Clear cookies (Settings → Clear Cache)
- Email manager to resend invite

**"Checklist doesn't load"**
- Refresh page (F5 or swipe down)
- Check WiFi/data connection
- Try different room/area
- Hard refresh: Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows)

**"Notification didn't show"**
- Unlock phone screen
- App might be running in background
- Check phone sound/vibration settings
- Refresh app (close and reopen)

**"Photo upload fails"**
- Keep photo under 5MB
- Try different photo
- Check WiFi connection
- Try uploading later
